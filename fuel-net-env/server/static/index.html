📖 Welcome to FuelNetEnv

FuelNetEnv is an OpenEnv-compliant reinforcement learning environment designed to benchmark state-of-the-art LLM autonomous agents on real-world global logistics and geopolitical crisis management.

🎯 Objective

Your autonomous agent is granted a \$30 Billion operating budget and acts as a crisis command logistics director. You must dynamically reroute global energy fleets to prevent devastating supply chain shortages when critical bottlenecks physically fail (e.g., a Strait of Hormuz blockade or pipeline attacks).

Click "Run Episode" to watch the baseline standard agent proactively attempt to fix global outages over a 30-day timeline simulation.

🏆 Scoring System

  • Fulfillment: Maintain >90% supply capacity distribution.
  • Speed: Reroute via the fastest geographic shipping channels.
  • Cost Efficiency: Procure fuel under strict budget parameters.
  • Reserves: Conserve sovereign strategic reserves prudently.
  • Penalties: Massive deductions applied daily for markets plummeting below 50% supply.
